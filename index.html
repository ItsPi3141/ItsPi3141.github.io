Alpaca Electron

Alpaca Electron is the easiest way to run the Alpaca Large Language Model (LLM) on your computer. It uses alpaca.cpp for backend, which means it runs on CPU instead of GPU. All you need is a computer and some RAM.

As for the frontend, it uses Electron (as stated in the name) and node-pty to interact with alpaca.cpp through the command line.

Models can be found in my Huggingface repositories

Intervoyage

Intervoyage is an image generation AI bot with no image generation limit, unlike the other popular bot that gives you a free trial of 25 generations. Though it is not as good, it still creates images with good quality.

This bot uses Openjourney, which is running on the Huggingface interference API, to create images.

Moto E 2015 (surnia)

The Moto E 2015 is a low-range Android smartphone released in February 2015. The Moto E 2015 comes in two variants, a 3G model and an LTE model. Surnia is the LTE model. It features a 4.5" 540 x 960 display. It is powered by a 64-bit 1.2GHz quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 processor. The phone comes with 1GB of RAM and 8GB internal storage. Other features include a 5MP rear camera, VGA front camera and a 2390mAh battery.

Samsung Galaxy S8/S8+ (dreamlte/dream2lte)

The Samsung Galaxy S8 is a flagship phone from the S-series, released in 2017. The standard S8 comes with a 5.8" screen with 1440 x 2960 pixels. The Exynos variant features an octa-core Exynos 8895 SoC and 4 GB of RAM. The phone has 64/128GB of internal storage and 4GB of RAM. The rear camera has a 12MP sensor and the front camera is 8MP. The 3000mAh battery comes with quick charging and wireless charging.
